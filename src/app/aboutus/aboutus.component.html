Welcome to Tutorai.Dev

Developed for a Google Developer Student Clubs (GDSC) hackathon, Tutorai.Dev is an innovative educational app designed to revolutionize the way users engage with learning materials. By integrating artificial intelligence, we offer personalized quizzes across subjects like Science, Mathematics, Social Science, and English, making learning interactive and fun.

Our Vision

Our team, comprising dedicated student developers and educators, aims to make knowledge accessible and engaging for everyone. With Tutorai.Dev, we're not just creating an app; we're building a learning community.

Technology and Innovation

Leveraging AI, Tutorai.Dev adapts to individual learning styles, providing instant feedback and tailored educational content. This project represents our commitment to harnessing technology for educational empowerment.
